Manufacturing Processes, VÉL403G

Spring semester 2021

Introduction

Manufactoring processes is a mandatory 6 ECTS course for all undergraduate Mechanical Engineering students in their third year. It is taught by Hafliði Ásgeirsson and we will be using Fablab equipment for our project work. The course description is as follows:

The objective of this course is to make the students aware of different manufacturing processes, help them to choose between different processes, given their pros and cons. An insight is given into production of finished goods from raw materials with different processes, e.g. moulding, forming, extrusion, machining and cutting processes. Permanent and non-permanent methods of assembling parts are explained along with different methods of surface treatments for strengthening and protecting against the environment. A practical assignment connects the course material with real time projects in metalworking.
